Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on ContentConnect?

Creating an account is simple! Click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner of any page. Choose whether you're joining as a writer or a client, fill in your information, and you're ready to go. You can then complete your profile to stand out to potential clients or find the perfect writer.

How does payment work on ContentConnect?

We offer secure payment processing through our platform. Clients fund their projects upfront, and payments are held in escrow until the content is delivered and approved. Writers receive their payments within 3-5 business days after project completion. We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

What if I'm not satisfied with the content I receive?

We have a revision policy in place to ensure your satisfaction. Most writers offer 1-2 rounds of revisions included in their base price. If you're still not satisfied after revisions, our dispute resolution team can help mediate a solution that works for both parties.

How do I become a verified writer on the platform?

To become verified, complete your profile with portfolio samples, pass our writing assessment, and accumulate positive reviews from clients. Writers who maintain high ratings and consistent quality work can apply for verification after completing 10 successful projects on our platform.
